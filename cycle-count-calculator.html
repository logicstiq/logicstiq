❓ Frequently Asked Questions
Is LogicstIQ really free? What is the catch?▾
There is no catch. All 31 calculators and the AI Demand Planner are completely free. No freemium tier, no expiring trial, no credit card required. We may earn affiliate commissions when users sign up for third-party tools we recommend — that is how we sustain the platform. All calculators remain free regardless.
Can I request a new calculator or feature?▾
Absolutely. Use the form above with topic "Feature request" and describe what you need. We built LogicstIQ based on real supply chain problems and we prioritise features that come up repeatedly from users. If enough people ask for something, it gets built.
Does LogicstIQ store my uploaded inventory data?▾
No. Files uploaded to the AI Demand Planner are sent to the Gemini API for analysis and immediately discarded. Nothing is stored in any database, no file is retained after processing, and nothing is shared with any third party. Close the tab and the data is gone permanently.
Can we partner with LogicstIQ or integrate your tools?▾
Yes. We are open to affiliate partnerships, newsletter features, and integration discussions. Email privacy@logicstiq.com with the subject "Partnership" and describe what you have in mind. We respond to all partnership enquiries within 3–5 business days.
I found a bug or a calculation error. What should I do?▾
Please report it using the form above with topic "Bug report". Include which calculator, what inputs you used, what result you got, and what you expected. We take accuracy seriously — all calculator results are validated against published supply chain formulas and we fix reported issues promptly.
