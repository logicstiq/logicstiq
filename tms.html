Free Transportation Management System (TMS) for Indian Ecommerce Sellers

LogicstIQ TMS is a free, no-login transportation management system built for Indian ecommerce and D2C sellers. Import your shipments from a CSV, compare live courier rates by pincode, optimise multi-stop routes, plan loads, schedule docks, audit freight invoices and track shipments by AWB — all in one fast, browser-based tool. No installation, no credit card, and your data stays on your own device.

What you can do with LogicstIQ TMS

  • Import shipments (CSV): upload, drag-drop or paste your orders and manage FTL, LTL, parcel, rail and air freight together.
  • Live courier rates: compare real prices and delivery times across couriers like Delhivery, Blue Dart, Xpressbees and DTDC by pickup and delivery pincode.
  • AI route optimization: sequence multi-stop routes and assign fleet to cut kilometres and freight cost.
  • Load planning & dock scheduling: consolidate LTL loads and book conflict-free warehouse dock slots.
  • Freight audit: catch invoice-vs-quote overcharges and recover money that quietly leaks from SMB shippers.
  • AWB tracking & AI copilot: follow live courier scans and ask questions about your freight spend, lanes and exceptions.

Frequently asked questions

Is the LogicstIQ TMS free?

Yes — completely free, with no login and no credit card. It runs in your browser and your data is saved on your own device.

What is a Transportation Management System (TMS)?

A TMS helps you plan, execute and track the movement of goods — shipments, carriers, routes, freight costs and delivery status — all in one place.

Can I import my own shipments?

Yes. Import from CSV by uploading, dragging and dropping, or pasting rows. Excel users can save their sheet as CSV and upload it.

Which couriers are supported for live rates?

Live rates come through the Shiprocket aggregator — Delhivery, Blue Dart, Xpressbees, Ekart, DTDC and more — compared by pickup and delivery pincode.

Do I need to install anything or log in?

No. It is a browser-based tool with no login or installation, and your data stays on your device.

Does it work across India?

Yes. Rate checks use 6-digit Indian pincodes, and route distances use real road data between major Indian cities.
