About LogicstIQ.com

LogicstIQ.com, Where Logistics Meets Intelligence, is a free and completely login free platform built for supply chain, logistics, and e-commerce professionals. We provide a complete suite of practical calculators and an AI powered Demand Planner that help you make faster, more accurate inventory and operations decisions, without spreadsheets, formulas, or guesswork.

What We Offer

LogicstIQ.com hosts over 20 free calculators covering the full inventory and logistics lifecycle. This includes Economic Order Quantity (EOQ), Reorder Point, Safety Stock, Inventory Carrying Cost, MOQ Optimizer, Dead Stock Recovery, Inventory Turnover, OTIF Rate, Fill Rate, Total Landed Cost, Lead Time Analyzer, COGS and Gross Margin, Logistics Carbon Footprint, GST Calculator for India, Warehouse Utilization, Pick and Pack Cost, and Supplier Performance Scorecard. Alongside these, our AI Demand Planner lets you upload an inventory or sales file and instantly receive a demand forecast, a reorder plan, slow mover alerts, and AI generated insights for the next 30, 60, and 90 days.

Who We Serve

LogicstIQ.com is built for supply chain managers, warehouse operators, logistics coordinators, procurement teams, e-commerce sellers, and operations professionals who need fast, reliable answers without relying on expensive enterprise software.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make complex supply chain and inventory math instantly accessible to every professional, regardless of company size or budget. We believe powerful planning tools should not be locked behind paywalls or steep learning curves.

Our Vision

We envision LogicstIQ.com becoming the everyday toolkit that supply chain and logistics professionals across India and the world turn to first, a single trusted place to calculate, forecast, and plan with confidence, built and continuously improved with real practitioner needs in mind.
