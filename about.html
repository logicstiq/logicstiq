🎯 Our Mission

Professional Supply Chain Intelligence — Free for Everyone

Most supply chain tools cost thousands of dollars per month or are buried inside ERP systems that only large enterprises can afford. The result: SME manufacturers, D2C brands, marketplace sellers, and small distributors are left making critical inventory and logistics decisions using Excel spreadsheets and gut feel.

We built LogicstIQ to change that. Every calculator, every formula, every AI-powered analysis — free and instant. Because every supply chain team deserves professional-grade tools, not just the Fortune 500 ones.

LogicstIQ is built specifically for the Indian supply chain context — GST on freight, e-way bill rules, Indian ERP formats like Tally, and the reality of selling on Amazon India, Flipkart, Meesho, and Blinkit — while remaining globally applicable for any supply chain professional anywhere.
