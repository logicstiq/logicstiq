Privacy Policy

LogicstIQ.com · Effective Date: 1 July 2025 · Last Updated: 1 July 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of LogicstIQ.com, including users in India (DPDP Act 2023), the European Union (GDPR), and globally. Please read it carefully.

🔒 The short version: LogicstIQ does not require login, does not store your inventory data, and does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Your uploaded files are processed for analysis and immediately discarded.

1. Who We Are

LogicstIQ.com ("LogicstIQ", "we", "us", "our") is a free supply chain intelligence platform offering calculators and AI-powered demand planning tools. LogicstIQ is co-founded and operated by Amit Kumar and Sachin Sirwani, based in India.

Contact: privacy@logicstiq.com

2. What Data We Collect

Data you upload (inventory files)

When you use the AI Demand Planner, you may upload CSV or Excel files containing inventory data (SKUs, stock levels, sales figures, prices). This data is:

  • Processed on our secure server (hosted on Vercel) to generate your forecast
  • Sent in summarised form to Google Gemini AI to generate written insights
  • Never stored, logged, or retained after your session ends
  • Never shared with any third party for commercial purposes
  • Deleted automatically when you close the browser tab

We do not collect, store, or process personal data such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or addresses through the use of our calculators or AI Demand Planner, as no login or account creation is required.

Contact form submissions

If you contact us via the contact page, we collect your name and email address solely to respond to your enquiry. This data is retained for up to 12 months and then deleted.

Cookies and analytics

LogicstIQ.com may use cookies for analytics (to understand how visitors use the site) and, once activated, for Google AdSense (to serve relevant advertisements). You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. If you are an EU/EEA user, by continuing to use the site you consent to our use of cookies as described.

Log data

Our hosting provider (Vercel) may collect standard server log data including IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited. This data is used for security and performance monitoring and is governed by Vercel's own privacy policy.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To process inventory files and generate demand forecasts and insights
  • To improve the performance and accuracy of our tools
  • To respond to contact form enquiries
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense (cookies)
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data for marketing, profiling, or any purpose beyond what is stated above.

4. Google Gemini AI

The AI Demand Planner uses Google's Gemini 2.5 Flash API to generate written insights. Only computed summary numbers (e.g. total SKUs, urgent reorder count, revenue at risk) are sent to Gemini — never your raw inventory file or any personally identifiable information. Gemini's use of this data is governed by Google's API Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

5. Google AdSense

LogicstIQ.com uses or intends to use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Google's use of advertising cookies is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Vercel — hosting and serverless functions
  • Google Gemini API — AI insight generation
  • Google AdSense — advertising
  • SheetJS (XLSX) — client-side Excel file parsing (runs in your browser, no data sent to SheetJS)
  • Google Fonts — typography (may log your IP to serve fonts)

We have no control over the privacy practices of these third-party services. We recommend reviewing their individual privacy policies.

  • Right to access personal data we hold about you
  • Right to correct inaccurate personal data
  • Right to erasure of your personal data
  • Right to grievance redressal (contact us at privacy@logicstiq.com)

Under GDPR (EU/EEA users)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: privacy@logicstiq.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Security

All data transmission between your browser and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Our servers (Vercel) implement industry-standard security measures. API keys and credentials are stored as environment variables and are never exposed to end users. We conduct periodic reviews of our security practices.

9. Children's Privacy

LogicstIQ.com is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, please contact us at privacy@logicstiq.com and we will delete it immediately.

10. Affiliate Links

LogicstIQ.com may contain affiliate links to third-party products and services. When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may receive a commission at no additional cost to you. All affiliate relationships are clearly disclosed near each affiliate link on the website.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of LogicstIQ.com after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
