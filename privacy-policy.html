Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how LogicstIQ.com ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit logicstiq.com (the "Site"). By using the Site, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or log in to use our calculators or AI Demand Planner. We do not knowingly collect your name, email address, or other personal identifying information unless you choose to provide it directly, for example by emailing us.

When you upload a CSV or inventory file to the AI Demand Planner, that file is sent securely to our server and to Google's Gemini AI service for the sole purpose of generating your forecast. We do not store, retain, or share the contents of uploaded files after your session ends.

Like most websites, we automatically collect certain technical information when you visit, including your IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, and time spent on the Site. This is collected through cookies and similar technologies, primarily via Google Analytics.

3. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use cookies to:

  • Understand how visitors use the Site (via Google Analytics)
  • Show relevant advertisements (via Google AdSense)
  • Remember your preferences between visits

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Doing so may affect some Site functionality.

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our Site. Google Analytics collects information such as how often users visit the Site, what pages they visit, and what other sites they used prior to coming to this Site. We use this information only to improve the Site. Google's use of this data is governed by Google's own Privacy Policy, available at policies.google.com/privacy.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings, or opt out of third-party vendor use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info/choices.
