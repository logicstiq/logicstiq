❓ Frequently Asked Questions
What is safety stock? ▾
Safety stock is buffer inventory held to protect against unexpected demand spikes or supplier delays. It's the difference between running out of stock and keeping customers happy during uncertainty.
How is Reorder Point calculated? ▾
Reorder Point (ROP) = (Average Daily Demand × Average Lead Time) + Safety Stock. When your stock level hits the ROP, it's time to place a new order so it arrives before you run out.
What service level should I use? ▾
For most FMCG and e-commerce: 95–97.5%. For your top-selling hero SKUs or seasonal peak periods: 98–99%. For very low-cost or slow-moving items where a stockout is acceptable: 90%.
Does higher safety stock mean better service? ▾
Yes, but at a cost. Higher safety stock reduces stockout risk but ties up working capital and increases holding costs. The goal is to find the right balance — our Z-score method helps optimise this scientifically based on your actual demand variability.
