📋 Procure-to-Pay guide

The procurement journey for Indian SMEs and e-commerce sellers. Generate every document here; file the statutory ones (e-Way Bill, GST) on the official portals.

The core loop

  1. Requisition (PR) — someone asks to buy; route for approval.
  2. RFQ → Comparative Statement — get quotes, compare on landed cost, pick a supplier.
  3. Purchase Order — order it. E-commerce POs can be pre-filled from the Demand Planner reorder recommendation.
  4. Inbound (ASN + Carton Manifest) — for Amazon FBA / Flipkart FF / dark-store inbound; upload to the portal yourself.
  5. Goods Receipt Note (GRN) + Inspection — record what actually arrived and its quality.
  6. 2-way / 3-way match — reconcile PO ↔ (GRN) ↔ Invoice; flag short delivery or over-billing before you pay.
  7. Debit/Credit Note + Payment Advice — settle differences and remit.
