❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is LogicstIQ completely free to use?▾
Yes — all 31 calculators and the AI Demand Planner are completely free. No login, no signup, no credit card, no usage limits. Free forever.
Does LogicstIQ store my inventory data?▾
Never. Your file is processed on our secure server and immediately discarded after your results are generated. Zero data is stored, logged, or shared. Close the tab and it's permanently gone.
What file formats are supported?▾
Excel (.xlsx and .xls), CSV, TSV, and TXT files up to 25MB. Real Excel files are read natively using SheetJS — no conversion to CSV needed. Supports exports from SAP, NetSuite, Tally, Zoho, Unicommerce, Amazon Seller Central, Flipkart Seller Hub, Meesho, and 15+ other systems.
Who is LogicstIQ for?▾
D2C brands, marketplace sellers (Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho, Myntra), manufacturers, distributors, C&F agents, 3PL companies, quick commerce suppliers, import/export businesses, and supply chain professionals globally.
Does it work for Blinkit and Zepto suppliers?▾
Yes. The AI Demand Planner has a dedicated Quick Commerce tab that plans by city, by dark store, and by time of day. It's the only free tool designed specifically for the hyperlocal replenishment challenges of Blinkit, Zepto, Swiggy Instamart, and BigBasket.
How accurate are the AI forecasts?▾
Accuracy depends on the quality and completeness of your input data. The AI Demand Planner uses Gemini 2.5 Flash with statistical forecasting models. All results are for planning reference only — always validate with your ERP or WMS before placing purchase orders.
Will you add more tools?▾
Yes. LogicstIQ is actively developed. Upcoming additions include a TMS (Transportation Management System) for carrier rate comparison and route optimisation, and a Pro plan for teams needing saved forecasts, scheduled reports, and API integrations.
