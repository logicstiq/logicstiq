Frequently Asked Questions

Is LogicstIQ completely free? ▼
Yes — all 20 calculators and the full AI Demand Planner are 100% free. No login, no credit card, no usage limits, no catch. Free forever.
Which ERPs and marketplaces does the AI Demand Planner support? ▼
The AI Demand Planner accepts exports from SAP, Oracle NetSuite, Microsoft Dynamics 365, Tally Prime, Busy, Marg, Zoho Inventory, Unicommerce, Cin7, Linnworks, Brightpearl, QuickBooks, Odoo, Amazon Seller Central, Flipkart Seller Hub, Shopify, Meesho, Myntra, Blinkit, Zepto, and any custom CSV or Excel file. It auto-detects column names — no reformatting needed.
What file formats are supported? ▼
CSV (.csv), Excel (.xlsx and .xls), TSV (.tsv), and plain text (.txt). Excel files are read natively — no need to convert to CSV first. Maximum file size is 25MB, supporting up to 100,000+ SKUs.
Is my data safe? Do you store my inventory data? ▼
Your data is never stored. Files are processed in your browser and on our secure server for AI analysis only. No inventory data is saved, logged, or shared with third parties. Each session is completely private.
How accurate is the AI demand forecast? ▼
Accuracy depends on your data quality and history length. With 3+ months of sales history, the AI uses trend detection and moving average methods that typically achieve 80–92% forecast accuracy. With snapshot data (single period), it uses velocity-based projection. Confidence scores are shown for every SKU.
Who is LogicstIQ for? ▼
LogicstIQ is built for supply chain professionals, inventory planners, demand planners, e-commerce managers, marketplace sellers, D2C brands, manufacturers, distributors, procurement teams, and founders who want professional supply chain tools without enterprise software costs.
Can I use LogicstIQ for quick commerce platforms like Blinkit and Zepto? ▼
Yes. The AI Demand Planner has a dedicated Quick Commerce tab that provides city-level demand forecasting, dark store replenishment planning, and hour-of-day demand peaks. It supports exports from Blinkit, Zepto, Swiggy Instamart, BigBasket, Flipkart Minutes, Amazon Fresh, and more.
Will you add more tools — like a TMS? ▾
Yes. LogicstIQ is actively developed. Upcoming additions include a paid cloud-based AI-powered Transportation Management System (TMS) for carrier rate comparison, route optimisation, and shipment tracking — designed for D2C brands, marketplace sellers, distributors, and 3PLs across India and globally.
