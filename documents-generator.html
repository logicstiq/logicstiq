Step 4 · Typical export order

  1. One-time setup: IEC, GSTIN/LUT, AD Code + ICEGATE.
  2. Send Proforma Invoice → receive buyer PO / LC.
  3. Prepare Commercial Invoice + Packing List.
  4. Book transport → e-Way Bill, insurance, Bill of Lading / AWB.
  5. File Shipping Bill on ICEGATE → get Let Export Order.
  6. Negotiate documents with your bank → FIRC.
  7. Self-certify eBRC on DGFT.
  8. Claim RoDTEP / Drawback.
