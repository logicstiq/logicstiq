📖 CGST vs SGST vs IGST — Explained
🏛️
CGST
Central GST — collected by the Central Government. Always paired with SGST for intra-state transactions. Each is half the total GST rate.
🗺️
SGST / UTGST
State GST — collected by the State Government. Applied alongside CGST for intra-state. UTGST applies in Union Territories.
🔀
IGST
Integrated GST — applied on inter-state transactions. Equal to CGST + SGST combined. Collected centrally and then distributed.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions
Is GST applicable on all freight charges in India? ▾
No. Goods transport by road through a GTA is taxable at 5% or 12%. However, transport of certain goods (agricultural produce, milk, salt, newspapers, defence items) is exempt from GST. Transport by non-GTA (like a private vehicle owner directly) may also be exempt.
Who pays GST on road freight — the transporter or the buyer? ▾
Under the Reverse Charge Mechanism (RCM), the recipient (buyer/consignee) who is GST registered pays GST on GTA services. The GTA itself does not collect or deposit GST in this case. This applies when the GTA has not opted to pay GST forward.
What is the GST rate for e-commerce logistics (Delhivery, Ecom Express)? ▾
Courier and express logistics services are classified as courier services and attract 18% GST. This includes Delhivery, Blue Dart, DTDC, Ecom Express, XpressBees, and similar B2C/B2B courier companies.
Can I claim ITC on freight GST? ▾
Yes, if the freight is for business purposes and you are GST registered, you can claim Input Tax Credit on freight paid. However, ITC is not available on freight for personal use or if used for exempt supplies. Always consult a CA for specific situations.
What GST applies to Amazon FBA / Flipkart fulfilment fees? ▾
Amazon and Flipkart charge 18% GST on all marketplace fees including fulfilment fees, referral fees, and storage fees. These are treated as services provided by the marketplace and are fully ITC-eligible for registered sellers.
